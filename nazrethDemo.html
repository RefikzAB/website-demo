Our Story

A Taste of Home, Far From Home

Nazreth Market was born from a deep love for Ethiopian culture, community, and cuisine. Named after the vibrant city of Nazreth (Adama) in the heart of Ethiopia, we bring the warmth and richness of our homeland straight to your neighborhood.

We are more than a restaurant — we are a market, a butcher, and a gathering place. Whether you're craving the communal joy of injera and wot, or you need the finest fresh-cut meat for your family meal, we have you covered with quality and care.

Every dish we serve honors the recipes passed down through generations. Every cut of meat is hand-selected for freshness and quality. Come as a customer, leave as family.

At Nazreth Market, our butcher counter is a cornerstone of what we do. We hand-select every animal for quality and freshness, then expertly cut to your exact needs. Whether for kitfo, tibs, or your own family recipe, our meat is unmatched.

We source fresh, hormone-free beef and lamb daily. Our butchers understand Ethiopian and East African cuts — tell us what you're making and we'll prepare it perfectly.
